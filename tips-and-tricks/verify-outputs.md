# Tip: Always Verify Clawpilot's Output

_Credit: Mason Kochis — shared in The A-Team 💪🥇🤓 chat, May 8, 2026_

---

## What to Watch For

Clawpilot can occasionally hallucinate or produce unexpected results:

- **Wrong names** — It may substitute a different first name for someone (e.g., same last name, different first)
- **Phantom files** — It may claim to have created or saved a file that doesn't actually exist

## Best Practice

Always do a quick spot-check after Clawpilot completes a task:
- Verify names match the actual people involved
- Check that files it claims to have created/saved actually exist in the expected location
- For high-stakes outputs (emails to customers, MSX updates), review before sending/confirming

---

## Notes

- This is a known LLM behavior, not unique to Clawpilot
- The more specific your prompt (full names, exact file paths), the less likely these errors are
