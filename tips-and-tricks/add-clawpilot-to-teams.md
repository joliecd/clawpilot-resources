# Tip: Add Clawpilot to Teams (Including Mobile)

_Credit: Ryan Otis, Pat Mehta — shared in The A-Team 💪🥇🤓 chat, May 7, 2026_

---

## How to Add Clawpilot to Teams

1. In Microsoft Teams, go to **Apps**
2. Search for **Clawpilot** and upload/add the app
3. Once added, you can chat with Clawpilot directly in Teams — no need to switch to the desktop app

## Works on Teams Mobile Too

Yes — the Clawpilot Teams bot shows up on Teams mobile as well.

## Why This Matters

You can run prompts, kick off automations, and get results delivered back to you in Teams chat without ever leaving your current context. Ryan Otis ran the meeting transcript automation entirely from Teams chat.

---

## Notes

- Keep your PC on if you want automations to run while you're away (the desktop app needs to be running)
- Teams chat interaction works the same as the Clawpilot desktop app
