# Tip: Permissions Issues — Check the Permissions Settings

_Credit: Ryan Perry — shared in The A-Team 💪🥇🤓 chat, May 8, 2026_

---

## Symptom

Clawpilot returns an error or refuses to perform an action due to permissions — e.g., accessing a mailbox, reading calendar, connecting to a tool.

## Fix

Check the **Permissions** section in Clawpilot settings. Some integrations (email, calendar, MSX, etc.) require explicit permission grants before Clawpilot can access them.

---

## Notes

- If you hit a permissions wall on a specific action, this is usually the first place to check
- Permissions may need to be re-granted after updates or re-installs
