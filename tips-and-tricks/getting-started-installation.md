# Tip: Getting Started — Installation & Access Request

_Credit: Benjamin Coughtry, Ryan Otis — shared in The A-Team 💪🥇🤓 chat, May 7, 2026_

---

## Steps

1. **Request access** → [aka.ms/clawpilot-request](https://aka.ms/clawpilot-request)
   - Access typically approves within ~5 minutes

2. **Download & install** → [aka.ms/clawpilot](https://aka.ms/clawpilot)
   - Do this after you receive access approval

3. **Run wild.** 🚀

---

## Notes

- You'll get a notification when access is approved — check your email or Teams
- If you're still pending after a while, check your request status at the same link
