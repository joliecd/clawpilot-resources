# Tip: Email Reply Safety — Test on Yourself First

_Credit: Quinn Meagher — shared in The A-Team 💪🥇🤓 chat, May 7, 2026_

---

## Context

When Clawpilot drafts or sends email replies, it will ask for approval before executing the `reply_to_email` command. Even when it says it will "save as draft," it can feel nerve-wracking to approve when real recipients are involved.

## Best Practice

**Test with yourself first.** Before using Clawpilot to reply to customer or external emails:
1. Ask it to draft a reply to a recent email to yourself
2. Review what it drafts and how it handles the approval flow
3. Get comfortable with the confirmation step before using it on real outbound emails

---

## Notes

- Clawpilot does save to Outlook drafts when it says it will — you'll see it in your Drafts folder
- The approval prompt is a safety gate — read it before clicking through
- Consider using "draft only, do not send" phrasing in your prompt for extra safety
